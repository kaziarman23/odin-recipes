Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna recipe is a rich and hearty Italian classic made with layers of pasta, creamy ricotta cheese, savory meat sauce, and melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil noodles and set aside.
  3. Cook ground beef with garlic and onion.
  4. Mix ricotta with herbs.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
