Fluffy Pancakes

Description

These light and fluffy pancakes are perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch. Made from scratch with simple ingredients, they’re golden, buttery, and absolutely delicious when drizzled with maple syrup or topped with berries.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (don’t overmix — lumps are okay).
  4. Heat a lightly oiled skillet or griddle over medium heat and pour about ¼ cup of batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit.
