Homemade Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe delivers a crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese — everything you want in a perfect pizza. You can customize it with your favorite toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or fresh basil.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, olives, basil, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add warm water and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for about 5–7 minutes, then let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  4. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a lightly greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly, then add cheese and toppings.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Slice and serve hot!
